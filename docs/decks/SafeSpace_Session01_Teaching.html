02

The executive mindset

Mindset 01

A nonprofit is still a business.

The only difference: you don’t own it, and you can’t profit from it personally. You still have to bring money in.

Mindset 02

You carry a double bottom line.

Impact and dollars. Almost everything you do has to serve both. That’s the job, and it’s harder than either one alone.

Mindset 03

From program leader to executive.

A different role, not just a higher one. It’s the step the two of you said you want to make.

Mindset 04

The donor is a partner.

You bring the work, they bring the funding, and you both want the same outcome. There’s no power dynamic. You’re in the same boat.
